Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Yohpal Affiliate Market

How are commissions calculated?
Commissions are calculated based on the product type and the merchant's terms. Typical rates range from 5% to 30% of the sale value.
When do I get paid?
Payouts are processed monthly, around the 15th of each month, for all commissions earned in the previous month that meet the minimum threshold.
Can I promote content on social media?
Absolutely! Many of our top affiliates promote primarily through social media channels. We provide social media-friendly links and tracking.
